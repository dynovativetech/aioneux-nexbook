## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Accessing the Portal

Open your web browser and navigate to the NexBook portal URL provided by your organisation.  
The portal is accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

### 1.2 Creating an Account

1. On the login page, click **"Create your account"** at the bottom.
2. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name**
   - **Email Address** *(used to log in)*
   - **Password** *(minimum 6 characters)*
3. Click **Sign Up**.
4. You will be redirected to your dashboard upon successful registration.

### 1.3 Logging In

1. Enter your registered **email address** and **password**.
2. Click **"Sign in to NexBook"**.
3. If your credentials are correct you will land on the **Dashboard**.

> **Forgot your password?** Contact your facility administrator to reset it.

## 2. Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home screen. It gives you a quick overview of your upcoming schedule and recent activity.

### 2.1 Welcome Banner

Displays a personalised greeting (Good morning / afternoon / evening) and tells you how many upcoming bookings you have.  
Click **"New Booking"** in the banner to go directly to the booking wizard.

### 2.2 Summary Stats

Four stat cards show at a glance:

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Total Bookings** | Lifetime number of bookings |
| **Upcoming** | Bookings confirmed or pending in the future |
| **Completed** | Sessions already attended |
| **Cancelled** | Bookings you or the admin cancelled |

### 2.3 Upcoming Bookings

Shows your **next 5 confirmed or pending** sessions, sorted by date.

Each booking card displays:

- **Date block** (month, day number, weekday) — highlighted in blue
- **Venue & Community** — e.g. *THE AQUILA SCHOOL, Dubailand Residence Complex*
- **Facility name** — e.g. *Swimming Pool*
- **Time slot** — e.g. *16:00 – 17:00*
- **Countdown** — *Today / Tomorrow / In X days*
- **Status badge** — *Confirmed / Pending*

Click **"View all →"** in the top-right of this section to go to My Bookings.

### 2.4 Activity Feed (Dashboard Preview)

Shows your **last 15 booking events** — a quick timeline of what has happened recently.

Each item shows the event type (Booking confirmed, Session completed, Booking cancelled, etc.) alongside the facility name, date, and status.

Click **"View all activities →"** at the bottom to open the full Activity Feed page.

## 3. Find a Venue

Use this page to browse and search all available venues in your network.

### 3.1 Searching for a Venue

1. Click **"Find a Venue"** in the left menu.
2. Use the search filters at the top:
   - **Country** dropdown
   - **City** dropdown
   - **Area** dropdown
   - **Community** dropdown
   - **Search text** — type any part of a venue name
3. Click **"Search"** (or the filter updates automatically as you select options).

### 3.2 Venue Cards

Search results appear as cards. Each card shows:

- Venue name and short description
- Location (community, area, city)
- Cover photo

Click on any venue card to open the **Venue Detail Page**.

### 3.3 Venue Detail Page

The detail page contains:

- **Image gallery** — hero slider with auto-slideshow every 3 seconds, left/right arrows, thumbnail strip, and a **"Show all photos"** button that opens a full-screen lightbox.
- **Venue name & address**
- **Facilities list** — available facilities with capacity, slot duration, and a **"Book →"** button
- **Amenities** — parking, WiFi, washrooms, etc.
- **Opening Hours** — weekly schedule showing open/closed times; today's row is highlighted in blue
- **Contact details** — phone, email, website, map link

To book directly from this page, click the **"Book →"** button next to any facility or the **"Book a Facility"** button at the bottom.

## 4. Book a Facility

This is a step-by-step booking wizard with 5 stages. You can navigate forward and backwards at any step.

### Step 1 — Find a Venue

1. Select your **Country** from the dropdown.
2. Select your **City**.
3. Select a **Community**.
4. Optionally type a venue name in the **search box**.
5. Click **"Search Venues"**.
6. Venue results appear as cards. Click **"Select"** on the venue you want.

> **Tip:** If you arrived here from the Venue Detail Page or via the Find a Venue page, the venue may already be pre-selected.

### Step 2 — Select a Facility

All active facilities at the selected venue are shown.  
Click **"Select"** on the facility you wish to book.

### Step 3 — Select an Activity *(optional)*

If the facility has linked activities (e.g. Lane Swimming, Aqua Aerobics), they will be listed.  
Select one, or click **"Skip / No Activity"** if the facility is for free use.

### Step 4 — Choose a Date

A calendar appears. Available dates are shown in blue; unavailable dates are greyed out.  
Click a date to select it.

### Step 5 — Choose a Time Slot

Available time slots for the selected date are displayed.  
Click a slot to select it (the slot turns blue when selected).  
Click **"Continue"** to proceed.

### Step 6 — Confirm Booking

A summary panel shows:

- Venue & facility
- Selected date and time
- Duration and price (if applicable)

Review the details, then click **"Confirm Booking"**.  
A success message will confirm your booking. It will appear in **My Bookings** with status **Pending** or **Confirmed** depending on your venue's settings.

## 5. My Bookings

View and manage all your bookings in one place.

### 5.1 Opening My Bookings

Click **"My Bookings"** in the left menu, or click **"View all →"** from the Dashboard's Upcoming Bookings section.

### 5.2 Filtering Bookings

Use the filter tabs at the top to view bookings by status:

- **All** — every booking
- **Pending** — awaiting confirmation from the admin
- **Confirmed** — approved and scheduled
- **Completed** — sessions already attended
- **Cancelled** — bookings that were cancelled

Each tab shows a count of matching bookings in brackets.

### 5.3 Pagination

Use the **"Show X per page"** dropdown (options: 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 40, 50) to control how many bookings appear per page.  
Navigate pages with the **‹ Prev** and **Next ›** buttons, or click a page number directly.  
The footer shows *"Showing X–Y of Z bookings"* so you always know where you are.

### 5.4 Booking Card Layout

Each booking row shows:

- **Date block** (month / day / weekday) in blue
- **Venue & Community, Facility name, Time**
- **Countdown** pill (Today / Tomorrow / In X days / Past)
- **Status badge**

### 5.5 Cancelling a Booking

If a booking has status **Pending** or **Confirmed**, a red **"Cancel"** button appears on the right.  
Click it to cancel. A confirmation prompt will appear. The status will update to **Cancelled**.

> **Note:** Cancellation policies may apply. Contact your facility for details.

### 5.6 Creating a New Booking

Click the **"+ New Booking"** button in the top-right of the My Bookings page to go directly to the booking wizard.

## 6. Activity Feed

A full timeline of your booking activity over the past and next **30 days**.

### 6.1 Opening Activity Feed

Click **"Activity Feed"** in the left menu, or click **"View all activities →"** at the bottom of the Dashboard Activity Feed section.

### 6.2 Reading the Timeline

Events are grouped by **month**. Each group has a grey header showing the month name and event count.

Each row shows:
- A **coloured left stripe** indicating status (green = Confirmed, amber = Pending, blue = Completed, red = Cancelled)
- A **status chip** with an icon and verb (e.g. *Booking confirmed*, *Session completed*)
- **Venue & Community** label
- **Facility** and optional activity name
- **Date and time** of the session
- **Status badge** and booking ID

### 6.3 Filtering by Status

Use the status chips at the top (**All / Confirmed / Pending / Completed / Cancelled**) to filter the timeline.  
Each chip shows a count of matching events in the current window.

## 7. My Complaints

Submit, track, and discuss complaints or requests with your facility team.

### 7.1 Opening My Complaints

Click **"My Complaints"** in the left menu.

### 7.2 Submitting a New Complaint

1. Click the **"+ New Complaint"** button in the top-right.
2. Fill in the form:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Category** | Select from the dropdown (e.g. Facility, Booking, General, Others) |
| **Related Booking** | Appears only for booking-related categories — select the booking from the list |
| **Title** | A short summary of your issue |
| **Description** | Full details of your complaint or request |
| **Images** | Upload up to 5 photos (JPEG/PNG) to support your complaint |

3. Click **"Submit Complaint"**.

Your complaint will appear in the list with status **Open**.

### 7.3 Complaint Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Open** | Submitted, awaiting review |
| **In Progress** | Admin is actively working on it |
| **Action Required** | Admin needs information or action from you |
| **More Info Required** | Admin has asked a follow-up question |
| **Resolved** | Issue has been resolved |
| **Closed** | Complaint officially closed |
| **Cancelled** | Complaint withdrawn |

### 7.4 Viewing and Commenting

Click on any complaint to open the detail view. You can:

- Read the full description and see attached images
- Read comments from the admin team
- Add your own comment (text + optional image) by typing in the comment box and clicking **"Send"**

> **Tip:** Use the comment section to provide additional information if the admin sets the status to *More Info Required* or *Action Required*.

## 8. My Profile & Account Settings

Update your personal information and change your password at any time.

### 8.1 Opening Account Settings

Click on your **name / avatar** in the top-right header.  
A dropdown menu will appear with the following options:

- **Edit Profile** — update personal information
- **Change Password** — update your password
- **Sign Out** — log out of the portal

### 8.2 Editing Your Profile

1. Click **"Edit Profile"** from the header dropdown.
2. The Account Settings panel opens on the **Profile** tab.
3. Update any of the following fields:

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **First Name** | Required |
| **Last Name** | Required |
| **Mobile Number** | Optional |
| **Phone Number** | Optional |
| **Email** | Read-only — contact admin to change |
| **Address** | Street address |
| **City** | City of residence |
| **State / Emirate** | State or emirate |
| **Country** | Country of residence |
| **Postal / PO Box** | Postal code or PO Box |

4. Click **"Save Changes"**.  
   A success message will confirm the update.

### 8.3 Changing Your Password

1. Click **"Change Password"** from the header dropdown (or switch to the **Password** tab inside Account Settings).
2. Enter your **Current Password**.
3. Enter your **New Password** *(minimum 6 characters)*.
4. Enter the new password again in **Confirm New Password**.
5. Click **"Update Password"**.

> **Security tip:** Use a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

## 9. Signing Out

1. Click on your **name / avatar** in the top-right header.
2. Click **"Sign Out"** from the dropdown.

You will be returned to the login page. Your session will be cleared.

> **Tip:** Always sign out when using a shared or public computer.

## 10. Tips & Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: My booking shows "Pending" — what does that mean?
**A:** Your booking has been submitted and is waiting for the facility administrator to confirm it. You will see the status change to **Confirmed** once approved.

### Q: Can I modify a booking after it is confirmed?
**A:** The portal does not currently support booking modifications. Cancel the existing booking and create a new one with your preferred time. Please check the cancellation policy with your facility.

### Q: I do not see any available time slots for a date I want.
**A:** All slots may already be booked, or the facility may be closed on that day. Try a different date or contact the facility to enquire about availability.

### Q: How do I know if my complaint has been addressed?
**A:** The status badge on your complaint will update as the team works on it. You will also see new comments from the admin in the comment thread. Check back on the My Complaints page regularly.

### Q: I uploaded the wrong image in a complaint. Can I remove it?
**A:** Once a complaint is submitted, images cannot be deleted. Add a comment explaining the error so the admin team is aware.

### Q: The venue gallery images are not loading.
**A:** Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl+R). Contact support if images remain unavailable.

### Q: How do I find venues in Abu Dhabi or Sharjah?
**A:** On the **Find a Venue** page, use the **City** dropdown to select *Abu Dhabi* or *Sharjah*, then refine with Area and Community filters.

### Q: I forgot my password.
**A:** Contact your facility administrator to reset your password. A self-service password reset feature will be available in a future release.
